Lucknow University Launches Part-Time MTech Course For Working Professionals

2 years ago
Follow us on:

Prerona Datta

Content Curator

New Delhi: The University of Lucknow has recently launched a part-time M.Tech course for working professionals for the academic session 2022-23. The online applications for the program will start soon after receiving the approval of the academic council.

As per the University of Lucknow, the newly launched MTech part-time course will be open for working professionals, BTech or Bachelor of Engineering degree candidates who wish to pursue their higher education at the university. 

FoET, University of Lucknow has started https://t.co/zlxytjRTfy part time course to facilitate higher education for working person. @profalokkumar @dswlkouniv @lkouniv @_poonamtandon pic.twitter.com/QeKTN3eI1L

— Faculty of Engg. & Tech., University of Lucknow (@foetlkouniv) May 2, 2022

Referring to the launch, University of Lucknow officials stated that the M.Tech Course is specially designed to introduce students to power systems in electrical engineering and industrial and production engineering in the mechanical engineering domain.

The University of Lucknow Program will be facilitated by the Faculty of Engineering and Technology department of the university. Classes for the same will be held for six semesters, from 6 pm to 9 pm on Saturdays and Sundays, the report further added. 

University of Lucknow M.Tech Course 2022: Key Highlights

Official Website  lkouniv.ac.in

Interested applicants who meet the eligibility criteria released by the university can apply for a part-time M.Tech Course under the University of Lucknow Admission 2022 by submitting their applications along with a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their organizations. 

Informing about the M.Tech course, the officials stated that the selection of applicants for admission seats will be done based on entrance exam scores and interview round, which will be conducted by the university. 

The University of Lucknow will soon release the admission process and guidelines for its part-time M.Tech program on its official website at lkouniv.ac.in. Interested candidates are advised to keep a tab on the university website for the latest admission updates.
